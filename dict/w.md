# W
## Genalphid English

1. [n, adj] A win, as opposed to a loss (L).

Example:

"W baby"

"Bro got that W drip fr fr"

2. Yes, positive.[^1]

Example:

"Alpha Sigma L rizz maxxing Skibidi, W L streak Tiktok alpha Ohio." (= "I don't like the weather here")
