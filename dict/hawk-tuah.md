# hauk tuah
## Genalphid English

Alleged "OG" pronunciation: /haːʔ tʼɔː/

"Spit on That Thang" pronunciation: /hɔkʰ tʼɤ̂ː/[^1]

Alternate pronunciation: /hɔk ˈtu.ə/[^2]

1. [onomatopoeia] spitting
2. (by extension) lubricant for questionable activities
